Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Good Reflections is developed by Jacob Good / Street Tribe. Your privacy matters to us and because the App is designed to run entirely on your device, there is very little data involved.

Data we collect

None. Good Reflections does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

  • No account or sign-in required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No network requests (the App works fully offline)
  • No access to camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photos

Data stored on your device

The App stores a local preference indicating that you have acknowledged the photosensitivity warning. This stays on your device and is never sent anywhere. That way the app only asks for acknowledgement for each instance it is opened.

Children

Good Reflections is not directed at children under 13. The App contains flashing, high contrast visuals that may not be suitable for photosensitive individuals of any age.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
