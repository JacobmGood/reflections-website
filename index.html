About

About this app

Good Reflections recreates the optical magic of video feedback the same effect you get when a camera films its own output entirely on your device using Metal and GPU shaders.

Two mirrored rectangles create a feedback loop. Drag to move them, pinch to resize, and rotate with two fingers to explore ever changing fractal shapes inside each window.

Built as a creative coding project by Street Tribe. No accounts, no ads, no data collection, just cool patterns and good music.
